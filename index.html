Welcome

Butternuts
good dishes

A little cook shop packed with all you need and more! Whether you’re setting up a new kitchen or adding to one, we can help. We carry pots and pans, small appliances, dishes, glassware, natural cleaning products, and gadgets – lots of fun and useful gadgets that you never knew you needed! All Clad, Cuisinart, Fiestaware, Vic Firth, Le Creuset, Keurig, and Vitamix are just a few of the lines we stock. Our “good dishes” include foods from local vendors and our own freshly prepared entrees and salads from our kitchen.

Kitchenware

An extensive line of cook and kitchen wares from cutlery, drinking glasses and utensils, to blenders, food processors and assorted electric devices.

Gourmet & Specialty Foods

We carry Beaveridge Bath and Body of Mirror Lake….wonderful all natural soaps and soy candles.

Try our private label line of Butternut’s salad dressings, stuffed olives, dipping oils, and marinades, salsa, and pasta sauces.

Stoneham Meadow brings us fresh local eggs from happy free range chickens. We have in season farm fresh produce from McKenzie’s Farm and Ledgewood Farm. They grow the best tomatoes!

We have a nice variety of imported and California olive oils, spices and herbs, jams, jellies, and relishes. Our assortment of olives, tapenades, salsas, chips, and crackers are perfect for party hors d' oeuvres. You can choose from a wide variety of gourmet chesses including our most popular Camelot’s Creamed Cheddar - perfect with pepper jelly and local cheeses from the Sandwich Creamery - their Brie is amazing, chevre from Via Lactea Farm in Brookfield, gouda and jack and from Taylor Brothers, Meriden, NH.

Our local specialty foods do not stop there. We have honey from the Carriage Hill Honey right here in town , great coffees from the Black Bear Micro Roastery of Tuftonboro, fresh salsa and dips from Mitchell’s. mead from the Sap House Meadery of Center Ossipee. nuts from Napoleon’s Peanuts of Meredith. jellies, jams and baking mixes from Fennelley’s and NH real maple syrup too.
