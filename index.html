About

Butternuts good dishes

IS A KITCHEN AND GOURMET SHOP on Railroad Avenue in beautiful

downtown Wolfeboro, New Hampshire. We are open year round.

Our in-season little farmstand is stocked with locally grown produce including red ripe tomatoes, cucumbers, fresh berries, apples, and beautiful flower bouquets.

Kitchenware

We carry a selection of all things you might need in your kitchen. We have cookware, small appliances, glassware, kitchen knives, bakeware, placemats and tablecloths, and so many useful kitchen gadgets!

    Some of our favorite brands are:
  • Fiestaware

  • Swiss Diamond

  • April Cornell

Gourmet & Specialty Foods

We LOVE local! We love to support our local farmers, artisans, and producers. Butternuts sells local maple syrup, chocolate, cheese, honey, mead, jams, seasonings, wine and baked goods. We have handmade wooden cutting boards, cheese boards, cards, soaps and more. We carry Centerville Pie Co. Pies! Handmade on Cape Cod. Delicious!

good dishes To Go

Our good dishes to go are prepared in our kitchen and include our homemade soups, salads, and quiche.
